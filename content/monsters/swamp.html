沼澤棲物

巴庫納瓦 (Bakunawa) 單獨, 大型, 聰穎, 毀亂, 強力

噬咬 (d10+3 傷害, 1 穿透), 16 HP, 2 護甲

近身, 及遠

特性: 兩棲

龍龜的姊妹，一頭強大的蛇后。有著十碼長的鱗片與肌肉，傳說當太陽從天空中消失之時，她將帶著無窮的飢餓從深淵中甦醒。被黑暗中的光輝吸引，就像所有蛇族一樣，巴庫納瓦卑鄙而鬼祟。她會先試著哄騙和誤導獵物，並只有在沒有其他選擇的情況下才會暴力出擊。不過，當她真的這麼做時，她下顎的可怕怪力足以粉碎任何膽敢行駛於沼澤的船身，也當然能夠輕易切開一或二副鋼鐵胸鎧。把你的財寶全部獻給這條貪婪的蛇吧，或許她會願意放你一馬。 本能: 吞噬

  • 使用謊言和幻覺引誘獵物
  • 朝著光源猛然襲擊
  • 吞噬

巴西利斯克 (Basilisk) 單獨, 囤財者

啃咬 (d10 傷害), 12 HP, 2 護甲

近身

「很少人能在看見巴西利斯克後存活下來並講述牠們的故事。懂嗎？看見過一隻巴西利斯克？一點巴西利斯克式的幽默……抱歉，先生們，我知道你們在找尋有用的情報。這很嚴肅，我懂。巴西利斯克，就算沒有那透過凝視把你的血肉化為石頭的能力，牠也是一隻相當危險的生物。牠有點像青蛙，有著球根般的眼鏡和六條肌肉發達，為跳躍而生的腿。牠也和鱷魚有些相似，有著可怕咬合力的顎和尖銳的利齒。全身覆蓋著石頭般的厚鱗且非常難以殺死。如果可以的話，你最好避開牠。」 本能: 創造出新的石像

  • 透過凝視將血肉變成石頭
  • 撤退回一座石雕迷宮中

黑布丁 (Black Pudding) 單獨, 不定型

腐蝕之觸 (d10 傷害, 無視護甲), 15 HP, 1 護甲

羽蛇 (Couatl) 單獨, 聰穎, 狡猾

光束 (d8 傷害, 無視護甲), 12 HP, 2 護甲

近身

特性: 羽翼, 光環

就彷彿是為了直接蔑視充斥在這個世界上腐朽和汙穢，眾神把羽蛇賜予了我們。就像是在說：「就算是在這個陰暗嚴峻的地方，也仍然存在著美好的事物」，牠們是振著珠寶羽翼飛翔天際的蛇，這些美麗的生物綻放著柔和的光輝，就如同透過彩繪玻璃的陽光。明亮、睿智、且冷靜，羽蛇總是知曉眾多，也看見過更多。你或許可以藉著與牠交易來得到一些好處。牠們希冀淨化、清洗、並讓這黑暗的世界變得更加美好。遺憾的是我們擁有的羽蛇實在太少……眾神畢竟是殘酷的。 本能: 淨化

  • 對某人或某地降下審判
  • 召喚神性力量來清洗淨化
  • 提供情報以換取服務

二重身 (Doppelgänger) 單獨, 狡猾, 聰穎

匕首 (d6 傷害), 12 HP, 0 護甲

近身

特性: 變形

牠們的自然形態，如果你曾經看過，可說是相當醜惡難看。牠就像一隻在決定自己要成為精靈、人類、或是矮人之前就停止了成長的生物。但或許，這也正是牠們之所以成為二重身的原因──變幻無形，卻沒有一個屬於自己的外貌，也許，牠們真正所求的也只不過是一個可以融入的地方。如果你出去外面的世界闖蕩，那麼當你終於返家，你最好確保你的朋友們仍然是你所認識的那些人。他們說不定其實已經被二重身取代，而你原本的朋友們也許早就死在了某處的井底。但另一方面，取決於你朋友原本的為人……這或許反倒是件好事也說不定。 本能: 滲透

  • 冒充曾被牠嚐過血肉的人的外型
  • 利用他人的身份以取得好處
  • 讓某人身敗名裂

龍龜 (Dragon Turtle) 單獨, 巨型, 謹慎

噬咬 (d10+3 傷害), 20 HP, 4 護甲

及遠

特性: 甲殼, 兩棲

巴庫納瓦有一個兄弟。她狂躁易怒且渴求黃金，而他則行動遲緩且厚重結實。若說她是利刃，那麼他就是盾牌。他是一頭巨大的烏龜，躺臥在淤泥和沼潭經歷了數個世紀的歲月，在他厚重的背甲上堆積著泥巴──有時則生長著樹木和灌木叢。偶爾，一整群誤入歧途的哥布林部落會在龍龜的硬殼上築起牠們的小屋，並烹調起牠們那不怎麼樣的菜餚。他咬合的下顎也許如冰川般緩慢，但那力道卻絕對能輕易扯開城堡的厚重磚牆。小心你的腳步。 本能: 拒絕變化

  • 堅決執拗地向前邁進
  • 發揮他那龐然身軀的全部力量
  • 摧毀建築物和房屋

幼龍 (Dragon Whelp) 單獨, 小型, 聰穎, 謹慎, 囤財者

元素吐息 (d10+2 傷害), 16 HP, 3 護甲

近身, 短程

特性: 翅膀, 元素血脈

什麼？你以為牠們全都身長一哩，沒有比這更小的尺寸嗎？當然，牠們可能不比狗大，也不比猿猴聰明多少，但一頭幼龍仍然能夠噴出一道足以把你鎧甲融化的地獄烈火，把你尖叫著埋進泥裡。牠們的鱗片確實比牠們那更大的表親要來得柔軟，但仍可以將那偏差的箭矢或刀劍彈到一旁。記住，體型並不是衡量力量的唯一標準。 本能: 增長力量

  • 建立巢穴做為牠力量的基礎
  • 請求家族的協助
  • 要求宣誓效忠

埃克克鳥妖 (Ekek) 成群

禽爪 (d6 傷害), 3 HP, 1 護甲

近身

特性: 翼肢

這些醜陋，乾癟的鳥人。在古老的過去，或許，牠們也曾經是如天使一般高高在上的種族，但如今牠們啃食老鼠，用猛禽般的腳爪捕捉深水中的魚，用針般的牙齒狼吞虎嚥。牠們聽得懂人類和矮人們的語言，但說得卻只比胡言亂語要好上一些，用嘲諷的笑聲模仿著牠們聽到的隻言片語。看著這頭與人類或鳥類是如此相像，卻又兩者皆非的野獸，實在是令人感到不寒而慄。 本能: 猛然襲擊

  • 從空中攻擊
  • 執行一個更強大生物的命令

火鰻 (Fire Eels) 成群, 微型

燃燒之觸 (d6-2 傷害, 無視護甲), 3 HP, 0 護甲

Hand

特性: 易燃油, 水棲

這些奇怪的生物並不比牠們世俗的親戚更大或更聰明。牠們擁有同樣兇殘的本性。跟牠們的親戚相比，牠們有一個優勢──一種從牠們皮膚滲出來的分泌物。這不僅讓牠們難以被抓住，更重要的是，牠們只要一個扭身就能點燃這玩意兒，讓覆蓋在水面上的大灘燃油熊熊燃燒，將獵物和獵食者一起烤個外酥裡嫩。我聽說這些滑溜溜的東西是製作抗火裝備的良好材料，但你必須先想辦法把它弄到手。 本能: 點燃

  • 讓某人或某物起火（即使在水中也一樣）
  • 吃掉燃燒中的獵物

蛙人 (Frogman) 成群, 小型, 聰穎

長矛 (d6 傷害), 7 HP, 1 護甲

近身

特性: 兩棲

Croak croak croak. Little warty munchkins. Some wizard or godling’s idea of a bad joke, these creatures are. They stand as men, dress in scavenged cloth and hold court in their froggy villages. They speak a rumbling pidgin form of the tongue of man and are constantly at war with their neighbors. They’re greedy and stupid but clever enough when they need to defend themselves. Some say, too, their priests have a remarkable skill at healing. Or maybe they’re just really, really hard to kill. 本能: To wage war

  • Launch an amphibious assault
  • Heal at a prodigious rate

海德拉 (Hydra) 單獨, 大型

Bite (d10+3 傷害), 16 HP, 2 護甲

近身, 及遠

特性: Many heads, Only killed by a blow to the heart

A bit like a dragon, wingless though it may be. Heads, nine in number at birth, spring from a muscled trunk and weave a sinuous pattern in the air. A hydra is to be feared—a scaled terror of the marsh. The older ones, though, they have more heads, for every failed attempt to murder it just makes it stronger. Cut off a head and two more grow in its place. Only a strike, true and strong, to the heart can end a hydra’s life. Not time or tide or any other thing but this. 本能: To grow

  • Attack many enemies at once
  • Regenerate a body part (especially a head)

狗頭人 (Kobold) 成群, 小型, 隱匿, 聰穎, 組織

長矛 (d6 傷害), 3 HP, 1 護甲

近身, 及遠

特性: 龍族聯繫

Some are wont to lump these little, rat-like dragon-men in with goblins and orcs, bugbears and hobgoblins. They are smarter and wiser than their kin, however. The kobolds are beholden slaves to dragons and were, in ancient times, their lorekeepers and sorcerer-servants. Their clans, with names like Ironscale and Whitewing, form around a dragon master and live to serve and do its bidding. Spotting a kobold means more are near—and if more are near then a mighty dragon cannot be far, either. 本能: To serve dragons

  • Lay a trap
  • Call on dragons or draconic allies
  • Retreat and regroup

蜥蜴人 (Lizardman) 集團, 隱匿, 聰穎, 組織

Spear (d8 傷害), 6 HP, 2 護甲

近身, 及遠

特性: 兩棲

A traveling sorcerer once told me that lizardmen came before we did. That before elves and dwarves and men built even the first of their wattle huts, a race of proud lizard kings strode the land. That they lived in palaces of crystal and worshipped their own scaly gods. Maybe that’s true and maybe it ain’t—now they dwell in places men long forgot or abandoned, crafting tools from volcano-glass and lashing against the works of the civilized world. Maybe they just want back what they lost. 本能: To destroy civilization

  • Ambush the unsuspecting
  • Launch an amphibious assault

梅杜莎 (Medusa) 單獨, 狡猾, 聰穎, 囤財者

利爪 (d6 傷害), 12 HP, 0 護甲

近身

特性: 將你變成石頭的注視

The medusa are children of a serpent-haired mother, birthing them in ancient times to bear her name across the ages. They dwell near places of civilization—luring folks to their caves with promises of beauty or riches untold. Fine appreciators of art, the medusa curate strange collections of their victims, terror or ecstasy frozen forever in stone. It satisfies their vanity to know they were the last thing seen in so many lives. Arrogant, proud, and spiteful, in their way, they seek what so many do—endless company. 本能: To collect

  • Turn a body part to stone with a look
  • Draw someone’s gaze
  • Show hidden terrible beauty

沙華魚人 (Sahuagin) 成群, 聰穎

Endless teeth (d6+4 傷害, 1 穿透), 3 HP, 2 護甲

近身, 強力, 毀亂

特性: 兩棲

The shape and craft of men wedded to the hunger and the endless teeth of a shark. Voracious and filled only with hate, these creatures will not stop until all life has been consumed. They cannot be reasoned with, they cannot be controlled or sated. They are hunger and bloodlust, driven up from the depths of the sea to ravage coastal towns and swallow island villages. 本能: To spill blood

  • Bite off a limb
  • Hurl a poisoned spear
  • Frenzy at the sight of blood

Great lumbering beasts, they live in places long since forgotten by the thinking races of the world. If you see one, drift by and gaze in awe, but do not wake the giant. 本能: To endure

沼澤蔓生者 (Swamp Shambler) 單獨, 大型, 魔法

Lash (d10+1 傷害), 23 HP, 1 護甲

近身, 及遠, 強力

特性: Swamp form

Some elementals are conjured up in sacred circles etched in chalk. Most, in fact. There’s a sort of science to it. Others, though, aren’t so orderly—they don’t fall under the carefully controlled assignments of fire, air, water, or earth. Some are a natural confluence of vine and mire and fungus. They do not think the way a man might think. They cannot be understood as one might understand an elf. They simply are. Spirits of the swamp. Shamblers in the mud. 本能: To preserve and create swamps

  • Call on the swamp itself for aid
  • Meld into the swamp
  • Reassemble into a new form

巨魔 (Troll) 單獨, 大型

Club (d10+3 傷害), 20 HP, 1 護甲

近身, 及遠, 強力

特性: Regeneration

Tall. Real tall. Eight or nine feet when they’re young or weak. Covered all over in warty, tough skin, too. Big teeth, stringy hair like swamp moss and long, dirty nails. Some are green, some gray, some black. They’re clannish and hateful of each other, not to mention all the rest of us. Near impossible to kill, too, unless you’ve fire or acid to spare—cut a limb off and watch. In a few days, you’ve got two trolls where you once had one. A real serious problem, as you can imagine. 本能: To smash

  • Undo the effects of an attack (unless caused by a weakness, your call)
  • Hurl something or someone

鬼火 (Will-o-wisp) 單獨, 微型, 魔法

射線 (低[2d8-2] 傷害), 12 HP, 0 護甲

短程

特性: 光之軀

Spot a lantern floating in the darkness, lost traveler in the swamp. Hope—a beacon of shimmering light. You call out to it, but there’s no answer. It begins to fade and so you follow, sloshing through the muck, tiring at the chase, hoping you’re being led to safety. Such a sad tale that always ends in doom. These creatures are a mystery—some say they’re ghosts, others beacons of faerie light. Nobody knows the truth. They are cruel, however. All can agree on that. 本能: To misguide

  • Lead someone astray
  • Clear a path to the worst place possible
